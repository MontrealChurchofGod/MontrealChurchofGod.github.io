"Surely, through the Lord Jesus, you can become a conquerer. Are you looking for a solution to your problems? An answer to your troubles?

This church is a place where you will find hope because the Lord Jesus comes in our midst when we faithfully gather before Him. You will get the joy of worshiping Him and fellowship with other believers as a family, when you come.

Please join our service on Sundays at 4:30pm. Our address can be found at the bottom of this page.

What is our mission?

We, the church are doing our best to help each other to have a close relationship with God through the Lord Jesus Christ. We believe in God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit.

The goal of our church is to represent Jesus, introduce the Lord Jesus to the world and to help its members to have Jesus in their lives to help us sail through all storms of life.

God's promise

God never promised the absence of storms in our lives. But He promises to walk with us through them.
