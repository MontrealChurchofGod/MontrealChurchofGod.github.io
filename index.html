Are you looking for a solution to your problems? An answer to your troubles? This church is the place where you will find hope.

Please join our service on Sundays at 4:30pm. Our address can be found at the bottom of this page.

What is our mission?

We the church are doing our best to help each other to have a close relationship with God through the Lord the Jesus Christ. We believe in God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit.

The goal of our church is to represent Jesus, introduce the Lord Jesus to the world and to help its members to have Jesus in their lives to help us sail through all storms of life.

God's promise

God never promised the absence of storms in out Life. But He does promise to walk with us through them.
